## 1. Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Google Chrome (recommended), Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, or Safari (latest versions)
- **Internet**: A stable internet connection is required
- **Screen**: Desktop or laptop screen recommended for best experience (the portal is responsive but optimized for larger screens)

### Accessing the Portal

Open your web browser and navigate to the UGCL Portal URL provided by your organization. You will be directed to the login page.

## 2. Logging In

### How to Log In

1. Navigate to the UGCL Portal login page.
2. Enter your **phone number** (10-digit numeric format).
3. Enter your **password** (minimum 6 characters).
4. Click the **Login** button.
5. Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the main dashboard.

### First-Time Registration

If you are a new user and need to create an account:

1. Click on the **Register** link on the login page.
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** - Your complete name
   - **Email Address** - A valid email address
   - **Phone Number** - Your contact number
   - **Password** - Must meet the following requirements:
     - At least 8 characters long
     - Contains uppercase and lowercase letters
     - Contains at least one number
     - Contains at least one special character
   - **Confirm Password** - Re-enter your password to confirm
3. Click **Register** to create your account.
4. You will be redirected to the dashboard upon successful registration.

### Changing Your Password

1. Navigate to the **Change Password** page (accessible from your profile).
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter your **new password** (must meet the strength requirements above).
4. **Confirm** the new password.
5. Click **Submit** to update your password.

## 8. Form Builder

The Form Builder allows administrators to create custom data collection forms without coding.

### Creating a New Form

1. Navigate to **Admin > Forms > New**.
2. Fill in the **Basic Information**:
   - **Form Code** - A unique identifier for the form
   - **Title** - Display name for the form
   - **Version** - Version number
   - **Description** - What the form is used for
   - **Module** - Which module the form belongs to
   - **Type** - Form type/category
3. Switch to the **Steps & Fields** tab to design the form:
   - **Add Steps** - Create multiple steps for multi-page forms
   - **Add Fields** - Add input fields to each step

### Supported Field Types

| Field Type | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Text** | Single-line text input |
| **Text Area** | Multi-line text input |
| **Number** | Numeric input |
| **Email** | Email address input with validation |
| **Date** | Date picker |
| **Select** | Dropdown selection |
| **Checkbox** | Multiple choice selection |
| **File Upload** | Upload files and attachments |

### Managing Fields

- **Drag and Drop** - Reorder fields by dragging them up or down
- **Edit** - Click on a field to modify its properties
- **Delete** - Remove a field from the form
- **Required Toggle** - Mark fields as mandatory or optional

### Previewing Forms

1. Switch to the **Preview & Export** tab.
2. See how the form will appear to end users.
3. Export the form definition as JSON if needed.

### Form Submissions

After a form is published, users can fill it out and submit responses:

1. Go to the form and complete all required fields.
2. Navigate through form steps using the **Next** and **Previous** buttons.
3. Click **Submit** when all steps are complete.
4. View submission history and details from the form management page.
5. Add **comments** to submissions for collaboration.

## 9. Workflow Management

Workflows automate the processing of form submissions through a series of states and transitions.

### Understanding Workflows

A workflow defines:
- **States** - The different stages a submission can be in (e.g., Draft, Submitted, Approved, Rejected)
- **Transitions** - The allowed movements between states (e.g., from "Submitted" to "Approved")
- **Notifications** - Alerts triggered when states change

### Workflow Designer

The visual Workflow Designer (available under **Admin > Workflows**) allows you to:

1. **Create States** - Define each stage in the workflow
   - Assign a **name** and **color** to each state
   - Mark states as **initial** (starting) or **final** (completed)

2. **Define Transitions** - Set up how submissions move between states
   - Choose the **source state** and **target state**
   - Define who can trigger each transition

3. **Configure Notifications** - Set up automated notifications
   - Choose which transitions trigger notifications
   - Define notification recipients and messages

4. **Validate** - Use the validation summary to ensure your workflow is properly configured

### Workflow in Action

When a form with a workflow is submitted:
1. The submission starts at the **initial state**.
2. Authorized users can trigger **transitions** to move it to the next state.
3. **Notifications** are sent to relevant parties at each transition.
4. The complete **workflow history** is tracked and viewable.

## 10. Document Management

Manage and organize documents within the portal.

### Uploading Documents

1. Navigate to **Admin > Documents**.
2. Click **Upload Document**.
3. Either **drag and drop** files or click to browse.
4. Fill in the document details:

   - **Description** - Brief description of the document
   - **Category** - Select or create a category

- **Statistics** - View document statistics from the dashboard

### Viewing Documents

Click on any document in the list to open the Document Viewer. From here you can:
- View document content
- Download the document
- Check document metadata

## 11. Project Management

Track and manage projects with budgets, timelines, and geospatial data.

### Creating a Project

1. Navigate to **Operations > Projects** or **Admin > Projects**.
2. Click **Create New Project**.
3. Fill in the project details:
   - **Project Code** - Unique identifier
   - **Project Name** - Descriptive name
   - **Description** - Project scope and details
   - **Business Vertical** - Associated business unit
   - **Start Date** and **End Date** - Project timeline
   - **Budget** - Allocated budget with currency (INR, USD, EUR)
   - **KMZ File** - Upload geospatial data file (optional)
4. Click **Save** to create the project.

### Project Dashboard

Each project displays:
- **Status Badge** - Active, On-Hold, Completed, or Cancelled
- **Progress Bar** - Visual completion percentage
- **Budget Utilization** - How much of the budget has been used
- **Timeline** - Start and end dates

### Project Map

If a KMZ file is uploaded, an interactive map displays:
- **Zones** - Geographic areas with polygon boundaries
- **Nodes/Waypoints** - Marked points of interest
  - Green markers = Start points
  - Red markers = Stop points
  - Amber markers = Waypoints
- **Navigation Controls** - Zoom, pan, and scale
- **Popup Information** - Click on markers for details

## 12. Notifications

Stay informed about important events with the notification system.

### Notification Bell

The bell icon in the header shows your notifications:
- A **red badge** displays the count of unread notifications
- Click the bell to open the notification dropdown

### Notification Types

| Type | Icon | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Workflow Transition** | Flow icon | A submission has changed state |
| **Approval Required** | Clock icon | An item needs your approval |
| **Approved** | Check icon | An item has been approved |
| **Rejected** | X icon | An item has been rejected |
| **Task Assigned** | Task icon | A task has been assigned to you |
| **Task Completed** | Check icon | A task you created has been completed |
| **System Alert** | Bell icon | System-generated notification |

### Priority Levels

Notifications have priority indicators:
- **Critical** - Red badge, requires immediate attention
- **High** - Orange badge, important action needed
- **Normal** - Blue badge, standard notification
- **Low** - Gray badge, informational

### Managing Notifications

- **Mark as Read** - Click on a notification to mark it as read
- **Mark All as Read** - Use the "Mark all as read" button to clear all unread
- **View All** - Click the link at the bottom to go to the full notifications page
- **Click to Navigate** - Clicking a notification takes you directly to the related item

### Browser Notifications

The portal supports browser notifications. When prompted:
1. Click **Allow** to enable browser notifications.
2. You will receive desktop notifications even when the portal tab is in the background.

## 15. Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Common Issues

**Q: I cannot log in to the portal.**
- Ensure your phone number is entered correctly (10 digits, numbers only).
- Check that your password meets the minimum requirements (6+ characters).
- Try clearing your browser cache and cookies.
- Contact your administrator if the issue persists.

**Q: I don't see a specific module or menu item.**
- Your access is based on your assigned role and permissions.
- Contact your administrator to request access to additional modules.

**Q: My dashboard is not showing any data.**
- Ensure you have selected the correct **tenant/business vertical**.
- Check that data exists for the selected date range.
- Try refreshing the page.

**Q: I'm getting a "Session Expired" or "Unauthorized" error.**
- Your login session has expired.
- You will be automatically redirected to the login page.
- Log in again with your credentials to continue.

**Q: How do I export data from a table?**
- Look for the export icon (download arrow) in the table header.
- Choose your preferred format: CSV, Excel, or PDF.
- The file will be downloaded to your computer.

**Q: My notifications are not appearing.**
- Ensure browser notifications are enabled (check browser settings).
- Click the notification bell to check for any unread notifications.
- Refresh the page to sync the latest notifications.

**Q: How do I switch between business verticals/tenants?**
- Use the **Tenant Switcher** in the header bar.
- Select the desired entity from the dropdown.
- The portal will reload with the new context.

**Q: The map is not loading in the project view.**
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection (maps require online access).
- Check that a valid KMZ file has been uploaded for the project.
- Try a different browser if the issue persists.

**Q: Form submission is failing.**
- Ensure all **required fields** (marked with *) are filled in.
- Check that file uploads do not exceed the size limit (100 MB).
- Verify your internet connection is stable.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
- Go to the **Change Password** page from your profile.
- Enter your current password and the new password.
- The new password must meet strength requirements (8+ characters, mixed case, numbers, special characters).

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered in this guide:
- Contact your **system administrator** for access and permission-related issues.
- Report technical issues to the **IT support team**.
